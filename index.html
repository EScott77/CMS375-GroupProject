About Harvest Bistro

A restaurant concept designed for both guests and management

Harvest Bistro is a modern restaurant website concept built for a database systems project. It shows how menu management, reservations, and restaurant operations can work together in one polished digital experience.

On the front end, customers get a polished restaurant site with food visuals, menu browsing, and reservation booking. On the back end, staff and admins can manage reservations and analytics.
