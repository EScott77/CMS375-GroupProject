About Harvest Bistro

A restaurant concept designed for both guests and management

Harvest Bistro is a modern restaurant website concept built for a database systems project. It is designed to show how menu management, reservations, and restaurant operations can work together in one clean digital experience.

On the front end, customers get a polished restaurant site with food visuals, menu browsing, and reservation booking. On the back end, the database can support reservation storage, menu updates, customer details, and operational management.
